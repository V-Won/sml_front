Laboratory Medicine

Laboratory Medicine

The Department of Diagnostic Laboratory Medicine plays a crucial role in ensuring the precise diagnosis of patients.
To achieve this, various specimens originating from the human body, such as blood, urine, and body fluids, are meticulously analyzed and interpreted. This process allows for the identification and differentiation of diseases, assessment of severity and progression, and plays a pivotal role in determining treatment strategies and predicting outcomes.

SML is promptly and accurately reporting results based on a state-of-the-art laboratory automation system.
Specialists in each field interpret the results and provide professional testing information through clinical advice and academic research, assisting in medical treatment.

Clinical Chemistry

The Clinical Chemistry department provides information for disease diagnosis and treatment monitoring by measuring the chemical components of various specimens, including blood, urine, and body fluids. Through the implementation of state-of-the-art automated chemical analyzers and rigorous quality control measures, we strive to deliver results that are both swift and precise.

Urinalysis

Urinalysis measures the physical and chemical characteristics of urine and is crucial in understanding the pathological conditions of the kidneys and urinary tract, as well as diagnosing endocrine disorders. Our state-of-the-art automated urine analyzer ensures reliable results.

Hematology

The Hematology department conducts essential blood tests for the diagnosis of blood disorders such as anemia, blood cancers, bleeding, and blood clotting disorders.
These tests include blood coagulation, transfusion, and hemoglobin A1c test. Our hemoglobin A1c test (HbA1C) has obtained NGSP Level 1 certification, ensuring accuracy in the test results.

Immunology

The Immunology department utilizes immunoassays (antigen-antibody reactions) to conduct serological tests for hormone quantification, tumor marker analysis, autoimmune disease screening, allergy testing, and diagnosis of various infectious diseases.
Our use of an automatic immunoassay analyzer significantly reduces testing time, ensuring prompt delivery of test results.

Radioimmunoassay

The Radioimmunoassay department conducts various examinations, including the diagnosis of endocrine disorders, utilizing radioactive isotopes as markers in a dedicated space equipped with highly skilled personnel and specialized facilities.

Special Chemistry

The Special Chemistry department has established a team of highly skilled professionals and equipped state-of-the-art specialized facilities to provide more precise inspection results with the latest specifications of specialized analytical equipment.
Our services encompass screening tests for inborn errors of metabolism, examinations of catecholamine-related substances, and analysis of heavy metals, among others.

Microbiology

Following the guidelines set by The Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI), we culture and identify causative pathogens of infectious diseases, conducting antimicrobial susceptibility testing to deliver precise and reliable test results.
Our facility maintains a dedicated and independent space equipped with specialized measures for laboratory safety and prevention of specimen contamination, ensuring a secure and meticulous testing environment.

Molecular Microbiology

In a dedicated facility, the Molecular Microbiology department employs state-of-the-art molecular technology to conduct examinations for bacterial and viral infections, while being equipped with essential features for contamination prevention, including dual-door access, negative pressure systems, and a designated PCR room.

Molecular Genetics

The Molecular Genetics department specializes in professional genetic testing, utilizing state-of-the-art analytical equipment and facilities tailored for genetic analysis.
Our offerings cover a wide range of genetic tests, including those related to hereditary diseases, genes associated with tumors, and transplant-related genetic examinations. Through a rigorous evaluation process, our highly specialized team ensures the delivery of accurate and high-quality test results.

Cytogenetics

Utilizing a cutting-edge automated karyotyping analysis system, the Cytogenetics department provides high-quality cytogenetic test results such as chromosome analysis and FISH Panel examinations for the diagnosis of inborn genetic disorders and chromosomal aberrations in tumor cells.

Specimen Management

The Specimen Management department manages the overall testing process by establishing a system that allows for precise handling, classification, and storage of specimens.
This ensures that specimens are accurately processed within the laboratory, facilitating efficient and prompt execution of testing procedures.
