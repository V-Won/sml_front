Jungbu Center

SML Jungbu Center

SML Jungbu Center is our regional testing center encompassing the Chungcheong and Honam regions with Daejeon at its core. Established in 2022 in the Yuseong-gu district of Daejeon Metropolitan City, the center has effectively narrowed the distance between customers, enhancing accessibility and convenience.

Conducting a wide range of specimen examinations—from diagnostic tests to pathological analyses—Jungbu Center has established an innovative automated testing system, GLP System, the first in the nation. Coupled with exceptional accessibility, this system enables us to deliver expedited and precise, customer-centric specimen testing services.
