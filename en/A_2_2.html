Busan Center

SML Busan Center, established in 2019 in Busanjin-gu, Busan Metropolitan City, is a regional testing center covering the entire Yeongnam region, including Busan, Daegu, and Ulsan, conducting a diverse array of specimen examinations, ranging from diagnostic tests to pathological analyses.

Built on exceptional accessibility, the center ensures the stability of specimens, operating a 24-hour system to provide the highest level of laboratory operations and delivering high-quality, customer-tailored testing services through stringent quality control measures.
