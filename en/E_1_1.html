Chairman Message

Chairman

Tae Kook Hwang

Samkwang Medical Laboratories will evolve into a new illustrious organization of healthcare services based on integrity and trust.
We sincerely thank you for visiting the Samkwang Medical Laboratories website.
Since its establishment in 1985, Samkwang Medical Laboratories has been leading the way in the field of diagnostic testing in South Korea, guided by the core values of 'Communication, Trust, Innovation, and Happiness', and practicing its mission of 'Smart Medical Life: Contributing to human health and happiness.’
Samkwang Medical Laboratories prioritizes the best quality by obtaining certifications from domestic and international evaluation agencies, offering over 4,000 laboratory medicine and pathology testing services. Through our three testing centers in Seoul, Daejeon, and Busan, we provide prompt and accurate test results to healthcare facilities nationwide. We are committed to continuously leading 'disease management and prevention-centered healthcare' through the proactive introduction of the latest automation equipment, state-of-the-art diagnostic technology, and continuous research as well as development.
Since its inception, Samkwang Medical Laboratories has continuously evolved by embracing changes in organizational culture and innovation that align with the shifting dynamics of the digital era, all aimed at creating new customer value. Moving forward, all Samkwang Medical Laboratories employees will continuously strive to leverage advanced IT systems and various digital convergence technologies such as big data and AI, to provide customers with a smart testing system that delivers faster and more accurate results.
We express our gratitude once again for your continued interest and love for Samkwang Medical Laboratories. We promise to continue providing the highest quality testing services based on trust and ethical management, always keeping customer satisfaction at the forefront.
The leap towards the future of Samkwang Medical Laboratories, dedicated to contributing to human health and happiness, will persist. We kindly request your generous advice and continued interest in us.
Thank you.
