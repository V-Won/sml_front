Chairman Message

Samkwang Medical Foundation is based on degree and trust
I will be reborn as a new medical service master.

Greetings,
Samkwang Medical Lab, based on the core values of Communication, Trust, Innovation, and Happiness, is a diagnostic testing institution leading the healthcare/biotech industry, contributing to human health and a happy life with Samkwang's unique value, Smart Medical Life.
Since its establishment in 1985, Samkwang Medical Lab has provided over 4,000 diagnostic medical and pathological testing services. These services are offered nationwide to hospitals and clinics through three major testing centers in Seoul, Daejeon, and Busan, all certified by domestic and international accreditation agencies, ensuring the highest standards. We are committed to delivering world-class healthcare services and continually leading in the field of "Disease management and Prevention-focused healthcare.” This commitment involves the proactive adoption of the latest medical equipment and advanced diagnostic technologies.
Since our establishment, we have operated on a singular management philosophy based on culture and innovation to push the bounds of value creation for the customer. In response to the digital age, Samkwang is dedicated to continuous improvement and development. All our regional testing center staff are actively working to integrate IT systems with big data, pushing the forefront of faster and more accurate results that meet the demands of the digital era.
We sincerely appreciate Samkwang Medical Lab's ongoing interest. We promise to continue standing for the customer, based on trust and an integral management system. Samkwang seeks continued support, advice, and collaboration as it endeavors to deliver the best testing services to contribute to human health and a happy life under the banner of "Smart Medical Life."
Thank you.
