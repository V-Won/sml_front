Mission & Value

Mission & Value

Samkwang Medical Laboratories considers the pursuit of 'Smart Medical Life' as the foundation of its management activities.
The shared mission among all employees is to become trustworthy and wise partners to customers, enabling society to enjoy healthy and happy lives.

Mission

Samkwang Medical Laboratories is contributing to the healthy and happy life of mankind through the practice of 'Smart Medical Life'

"We will contribute to a happier and healthier world through
the value that only Samkwang can offer"

Core Value

All employees of Samkwang Medical Laboratories adhere to the four core values of 'Communication, Trust, Innovation, and Happiness' as guiding principles and actively seek to embody them in their actions.

SML Core Value
  • Communication

    Open-mindedly exchanging opinions with others
    to create the best possible outcomes together.

  • Trust

    The company and its employees work together
    to achieve common goals.

  • Innovation

    Breaking free from past approaches,
    always pursuing innovation.

  • Happiness

    Always giving our best for the happiness
    of the organization and customers.
