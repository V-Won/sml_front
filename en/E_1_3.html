Mission & Value

The shared vision of all employees at SamKwang Medical Lab is to become trusted and wise companion to customers, enabling members of society to enjoy healthy and happy lives.

MISSION

Samkwang Medical Lab is contributing to the healthy and happy human lives through the practice of 'Smart Medical Life.'

  • SMART
  • MEDICAL
  • LIFE
Core Value

All employees at Samkwang Medical Lab aim to adopt and actively practice the four core values of 'Communication, Trust, Innovation, and Happiness' as guiding principles for their actions.

  • Communication

    Open-mindedly exchanging opinions with others to create the best possible outcomes together.

  • Trust

    The company and its employees work together to achieve common goals.

  • Innovation

    Breaking free from past approaches, always pursuing innovation.

  • Happiness

    Always giving our best for the happiness of the organization and customers.
